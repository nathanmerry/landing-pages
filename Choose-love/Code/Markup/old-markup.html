Objective of This Project

The Primary Goal of this project show people that they are not forgotten by the rest of the world, and that there is hope for them, with a particular focus on less developed countries.

Funding and visiting schools, outreaching to prisons, prostitutes and those with drug/alcohol problems and giving medical and sanitary aid are just a few of the things we do to show love to past communities, with much more to come!

Why volunteer with choose love

Choose Love is a small charity comprised of non-paid volunteers with big hearts. The more people's life we positively influence and make a difference to, the more hungry we get to make an even bigger impact, whether they are an innocent child wanting an education, or an adult in prison.

We may not be as big as Oxfam or Save the Children, but our appetite to make a difference in this world is just as potent.

If you want to be apart of a family that truly cares about giving hope to hopeless, join us on our mission. Whether it is through fundraising, coming on the trip, or lending out skills useful for moving the charity forward. All work will move us towards the primary goal of showing love to communities.
